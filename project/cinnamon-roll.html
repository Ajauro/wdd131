Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Dough Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 package active dry yeast (10g)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

Filling Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup melted butter

Frosting Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Preparation Steps

Dough Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix sugar, melted butter, salt, and eggs.
  3. Add the dissolved yeast mixture to the sugar and egg mix.
  4. Gradually mix in flour until a soft dough forms.
  5. Transfer dough onto a floured surface and knead for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Assembly

  1. After dough has risen, roll it out into a 16x10 inch rectangle.
  2. Spread melted butter over the dough.
  3. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle evenly over the buttered dough.
  4. Roll up the dough tightly, starting from the long side.
  5. Cut rolled dough into 12 slices and place them in a greased baking pan.

Baking

  1. Cover rolls with a clean cloth and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (180Â°C).
  3. Bake rolls for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

Frosting Preparation

  1. While rolls are baking, beat together softened butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until creamy.
  2. Spread frosting over warm cinnamon rolls.

Tips

  • For added flavor, sprinkle chopped nuts or raisins over the cinnamon-sugar mixture before rolling.
  • Serve cinnamon rolls warm to enjoy their soft texture and delicious aroma.

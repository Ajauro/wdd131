Site Name

Name: All the Recipes I've Loved Before

This name represents a recipe site for people who like to cook, or who want to learn through recipes

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to present recipes for healthy, normal, easy, fit, vegan and cultural foods.

Scenarios

On the site users will find a variety of recipes, and the main thing is that through a "donate your recipe" form users will be able to contribute to the growth of the site, i.e. they will register on the site and be able to send in their own recipes, and we will collect the information and add it to the site, with the user's author information. We'll have some navigation tabs to guide you to healthy, vegan, international recipes, etc. Site information, help and contact tabs. As the main users use mobile devices, it will have a good design for navigation.
